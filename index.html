Welcome to the Greenlaws Archive

This site is dedicated to reintroducing and preserving the work originally featured on the Greenlaws website, along with materials copyrighted by Louisiana State University and other key resources related to tree and landscape ordinances. The site may be periodically updated to reflect ongoing efforts to share, expand upon, and build on these contributions. It is maintained by Bennett Smithhart, a Professional Urban Planner and a California Licensed Landscape Architect.
